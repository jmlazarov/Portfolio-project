A little about me...

Based in Ontario Canada, my journey into coding started with the building of my own artwork gallery HTML website. I then collaborated on several client site projects and also learned to use WordPress, with which I built and maintain posthumous sites for my paternal grandparents, artist Boschka and poet Irving Layton. Currently I'm also the Webmaster for a client's site on Squarespace, and am in the process of learning JavaScript and version control.

In building websites and collaborating on several site projects, I realized I'd found something that gave me the creativity & versatility I was looking for in my work. I'm now happy to be able to offer Website Development services full-time, and I look forward to using my organizational skills and eye for detail & design to write clean, elegant and responsive code that helps to improve existing sites or bring new client projects to life.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is on writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML by hand to ensure that the content is accessible, easy to read, trouble-shoot and collaborate on.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and up to date with current best practices.

Accurate Design Conversion

You can trust me to accurately convert your design vision into robust, user-friendly, responsive and beautiful webpages.
